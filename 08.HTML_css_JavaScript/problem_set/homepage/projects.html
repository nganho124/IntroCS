Projects

01 - Supply Chain Performance Evaluation

Conducted an end-to-end supply chain performance evaluation for Australian dairy company, focusing on sales & service, transportation and inventory performance. Built a standardized KPI framework and validated ERP data to ensure comparability across regions and business units.

Impact:

  • Identified cost-to-serve and service-level gaps with targeted KPI
  • Enabled management to prioritize operational improvement initiatives using data-driven insights
  • Improved transparency and consistency in performance reporting

02 - Future Network Design

Led data-driven network design analyses to evaluate future-state distribution scenarios, including decentralized network, warehouse consolidation, cross-docking, and product re-allocation. Cleaned and structured large-scale ERP, transport, and demand data to ensure reliable optimization modeling.

Impact:

  • Recommended network redesigns that reduced logistics cost and improved service level leadtime
  • Delivered multi-million AUD savings through optimized DC footprint and flow design
  • Increased inventory resilience and reduced time-to-serve across the distribution network

03 - Market Research for Factory Investment

Performed market research and supply chain feasibility analysis to support factory investment and capacity expansion decisions. Assessed demand distribution, logistics cost, supplier proximity, and service requirements across candidate regions.

Impact:

  • Quantified cost–service trade-offs for alternative factory locations and production scenarios
  • Supported leadership decision-making with integrated market, cost, and supply chain insights
  • Reduced investment risk by aligning network design with long-term demand and growth strategy

04 - Supply Chain Dashboard Creation

Designed and built interactive supply chain dashboards using Python (Streamlit) and R Shiny to visualize logistics, inventory, and SKU performance. Automated data processing to replace manual Excel-based reporting.

Impact:

  • Improved decision-making speed through real-time KPI visibility and scenario exploration
  • Increased stakeholder adoption by translating complex analyses into intuitive dashboards
  • Reduced reporting effort and improved data consistency across supply chain functions
